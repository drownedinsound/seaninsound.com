Why This Audience

They Don't Just Listen. They Do Something.

Reach matters. Engagement quality is rarer. DiS listeners consistently outperform industry benchmarks on every meaningful signal - because they're music fans who came looking for something, not passive scrollers who stumbled in.

57%
Listen-through rate
The industry average is 40%. DiS listeners complete nearly 60% of every episode on average - a reliable signal that this is an audience actively engaged with the content, not running it in the background. They hear the partnership mentions.
44%
Newsletter open rate
The industry average is around 21%. DiS's newsletter reaches 8,000+ subscribers at more than double that rate. These are readers who opted in, stay opted in, and actively seek out DiS's recommendations - including partner content.
50%
Of all downloads come via Apple Podcasts - 3.5x Spotify's share. Apple listeners are comfortable paying for premium services.
81%
Female viewers on @drownedinsoundorg TikTok (410K viewers). Main account (@drownedinsounduk) is 60% female. The hi-res audio market skews heavily male - DiS reaches the growth demographic.
54%
Of TikTok traffic is search-driven - meaning listeners are finding DiS content intentionally, not just because an algorithm served it.

Proof of Impact

DiS Listeners Don't Just Hear About Change. They Participate In It.

Every campaign DiS has platformed has had a measurable outcome - from listener behaviour to legislative change. This is the evidence that the audience acts on what it hears.

↑ Spotify boycott (#CutOffTheSpigot) - six episodes, two of which are the most-downloaded in the podcast's history. This audience is already moving toward fairer alternatives. Directly relevant to audio streaming partners.
↑ Ticket resale cap - three episodes. Draft legislation in the King's Speech, May 2026. DiS covered it before Parliament noticed.
↑ UK Artist Touring Fund (UKAT / FAC) - two-part series timed to the application window. Fund applications opened during the broadcast window. With David Martin (FAC CEO) and Roxanne de Bastion.
↑ The Ticket Bank - over 1 million free tickets given to people who can't afford live music. Jan 2026.
↑ Music Industry Misconduct / On Wednesdays We Wear Black - accountability reporting with Sophie K and Yasmin Summan. Oct 2025.
↑ Sounds Like Change - first show in the DiS podcast network. Mental health and music. Hosted by Ariana Alexander-Sefre. Launched April 2026.

About the Host

Sean Adams

The DiS Podcast is hosted and produced by Sean Adams, who founded Drowned in Sound in 2000 and has been part of the independent music ecosystem ever since.

Photo: Jen McCord / SheSaidSo

He also runs The Sounding, a music management consultancy whose clients include The Anchoress. He released debut singles by Kaiser Chiefs and Bat for Lashes on DiS Recordings, wrote a fortnightly column for The Sunday Times, and created viral content for BBC Radio 6 Music. He has AuDHD and works approximately two days a week on DiS - which means every episode and every partnership is a considered choice, not content production.
